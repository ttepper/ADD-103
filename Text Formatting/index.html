Spider-Man

Spider-Man (2002) starring Tobey Maguire is a great superhero movie that tells the origin story of Peter Parker, an ordinary teenager who gains incredible abilities after a spider bite. The film has exciting action scenes, a strong emotional story, and a memorable villain in the Green Goblin. With a mix of fun, drama, and great performances, it's a classic that helped shape modern superhero films.

Characters: Peter Parker (Spider-Man), Mary Jane Watson, Aunt May and Uncle Ben, Norman Osborn (Green-Goblin)

Peter Parker is the main character in the film and is alter-ego is Spider-Man. MJ (Mary Jane Watson) is Peter's love interest/family friend. Aunt May and Uncle Ben are Peter's legal guardians. Norman Osborn/Green Goblin is the main antagonist in the film whos goal is to kill Spider-Man and everyone he loves.

Quote from Uncle Ben that says: "With great power comes great responsibility."
